Overview

This project involved the application of public relations and communication principles to conceptualise and develop a comprehensive PR framework and narrative for a fashion brand. The aims and objectives were to understand the role of PR & communication strategy in building a brand identity and effective narrative; apply PR knowledge, to implement an effective communication strategy; and understand and analyse analog and digital communication mediums to establish a successful narrative.

Key deliverables included a PR & Communication Strategy Proposal based on the RACE (Research, Action, Communication, Evaluation) model, a detailed Brand Narrative outlining the brandâ€™s ethos and target market, a Stakeholder Management List, a Marketing Calendar, and a Campaign Design encompassing campaign ideas, narrative, and agenda. Furthermore, the project scope comprised a Press Kit with an Invite, a Press Release, a Social Media Page and a Fashion Promo Video.
